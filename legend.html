What need have I for the fictions of other men? Does not my own life suffice to echo through the halls of memory until the end of time? I shall build my mythos through will.

Avalokitesvara looks upon me in her manifestation as Guanyin Boddhisattva. I charge her amulet granting "lifetime peace, smooth work" through the giving of charitable alms.

I, of the dual nature, Esil and Isar.

Know the light, for it we fight. Keep in dark, to make our mark.

I will hide my secret sword, smile my secret smile.

The best practice becomes unnecessary.

I choose to hide as much muscle as possible. I will become as strong as possible while looking as slender as possible. My strength will come as a surprise, for in surprise I find yet more strength.

By Matthew Bunday. ILUVU.
