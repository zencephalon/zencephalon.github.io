Beliefs of Playism:

  1. We live inside a game.
  2. We all chose to play this game.
  3. We chose who we would play this game as.
  4. We live on after the game ends.

Benefits of Playism:

  1. We don't take our lives too seriously, knowing that the Game will end one day.
  2. We enjoy the game and we help others enjoy it too.
  3. We don't pity anyone, knowing that they chose a harder difficulty setting. Instead we just help them play.
  4. We acknowledge different play styles.
By Matthew Bunday. ILUVU.
