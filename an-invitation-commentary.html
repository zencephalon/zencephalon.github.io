An Invitation is yet another sequel to Separation. Because why not make it a trilogy? I ended up really enjoying writing from Aliza’s perspective, so I decided to do it again. I wasn’t really “over” the girl who inspired Aliza, so I had to write a story giving Separation a more hopeful ending. In this story I was experimenting with characterization, and I tried to create as varied of a cast I could in limited space.

By Matthew Bunday. ILUVU.
